How long packet coffee can last?

Our packages of instant coffee can last up to two years from the date of manufacture.

Why is espresso expensive than regular coffee?

The grind of espresso coffee has to standard (no boulders or fines), so better quality and more expensive grinders are used.

What is the difference between roasted and dark roasted coffee?

Roasts are well-known for their bright acidity, while dark roasts provide a bold and robust flavor with rich, smoky finish.

Is coffee healthy?

Coffee contains antioxidants and other active substances that may reduce internal inflammation and protect against diseases.
